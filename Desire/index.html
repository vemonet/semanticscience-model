Class: Desire

desire is a strong emotion of wanting to have something or wishing for something to happen.

URI: sio:Desire

Parents

  • is_a: Interest - interest is the emotion of wanting to know or learn about something or someone.

Children

  • Curiosity - curiosity is the strong desire to know or learn something.
  • Intent - intent is a desire to realize a particular outcome.
  • Lust - lust is the strong desire for sex.
  • Passion - passion is the intense desire for something.
